    Concept

    SerenityHere was developed by SerenityHere Core Team of IIIT Kottayam. It was developed with a thought that the people who are introverts or the people who face difficulties on expressing their feelings and emotions, can do the mentioned with another way without any hesitation and lack of confidence because you will have to post your confessions anonymously. So, people will not be exposed to your identity and you can confess to anyone just by mentioning that person’s e-mail Id. We will send a message to him/her stating the confession without disclosing your identity. And believe us….! We will really not disclose your identity. And people can present their views on your posted confession. SerenityHere also gives you a very gripping and captivating feature which will empower you with a function of our thought where you can post any question related to your any theme of your life like any academic problems, time management problems, family issues, etc. You will get different solutions by different people and you can go with the best one.

    The main fact is that people will not be able to judge you on your any higher aspects of life like religion, caste or on any type of your background.

    Please be sure to not to post any sensitive content. Our system will automatically censor the sensitive contents but then also you are advised to abide by the social media rules and regulations and maintain the decorum of the site.
