    About Us

    SerenityHere team is led by four team members of IIIT KOTTAYAM community who all are the first year B.Tech students of C.S.E. SerenityHere concept is an idea pitched by Shashank Srivastava, one of our core team member, which led us realise the fact that many people hesitate in sharing their feelings, emotions and deeds only by considering the response of the society or that particular person for whom the emotions is applicable. So, we made a platform where you can confess to a particular person or a group without exposing your identity to the concerned person or group. We have also designed a platform where you can ask any doubts regarding any sort of your life.


    Our upcoming features and updates:

    Our team is working continuously to add various additional features like in future, you'll will be able to post to a particular community. We are also going to upgrade this platform to make this website more secure and free of cuss words for your convenience. We are working looking on various new designs and lookouts which will upgrade your experience on this platform.
